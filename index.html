About Our Clinic

CheckMate is a modern dermatology clinic where science, expertise and refined care meet. Built on international standards, it offers medical dermatology, advanced aesthetics and comprehensive sexual health services. CheckMate is the home of dermatology, reimagined as an experience.

Dr. Ismaël Maatouk is a dermatologist and global STI expert with over 15 years of clinical and public-health leadership. He led Lebanon's National STI Response, advancing HIV and STI services and improving access for marginalized communities. From 2022 to 2026, he served at the WHO in Geneva, directing global gonorrhoea resistance surveillance and supporting evidence based STI control worldwide.
